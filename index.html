Soul — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-22 · Applies to the Soul mobile app (iOS & Android) and its backend services.

Soul lets you leave a short voice, video, or photo message tied to a physical place — either as a private gift for one person who hears it when they are there, or as a public message anyone standing at that spot can discover. Because Soul is about places and feelings, it necessarily handles sensitive information. This policy explains, in plain language, what we collect, why, where it lives, and the control you have over it. Soul is built to be data-minimising and EU-resident by design.

We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, run third-party advertising or cross-app tracking SDKs, build advertising profiles, or use your recordings to train AI models.

3. Sensitive (“special-category”) data — your feelings, voice, face, location

Some of what Soul handles can reveal sensitive things about you — your chosen emotion, recordings that may include your voice or face, and your precise location. Under GDPR Article 9 the emotion data gets extra protection.

  • We process it only with your explicit consent, given when you choose a feeling and when you record and send a message.
  • The emotion is always chosen by you — Soul does not infer your mood from your voice, face, or words.
  • You can withdraw consent any time by deleting the message (§6) or your account.

4. Where your data lives

All personal data — accounts, messages, location, and media files — is stored on servers in the European Union: database, authentication, and file storage are provided by Supabase (EU region). We do not transfer your personal data outside the EU/EEA for storage.

5. Who we share data with (processors)

  • Supabase (EU) — database, authentication, and file (recording) storage.
  • Expo — to deliver push notifications (a push token only; no message content).
  • Apple / Google — to show the map and to convert a message's coordinates into a place name (reverse geocoding), plus operating-system push delivery and app distribution. Only the relevant coordinates are sent, for that purpose.

Each processor acts on our instructions under a data-processing agreement. We may disclose data if legally required, or to protect users' safety.

6. Your control and your rights

Any time you can delete a message you sent (removes it and its recording), delete your account (in-app), and turn off location or notifications in device settings. Under GDPR you also have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, portability, and to withdraw consent. To exercise a right, email kvaraciejus@gmail.com; we respond within one month. You may also complain to Lithuania's State Data Protection Inspectorate (VDAI — ada.lt) or your local EU authority.

Deleting your account and data

You can delete your Soul account and all associated data at any time, in two ways:

  • In the app: open the menu (top of the map) and tap Delete account. This permanently removes your account, the messages and recordings you created, and your personal data from our servers.
  • By email: write to kvaraciejus@gmail.com from your account email address and ask us to delete your account. We complete deletion within 30 days.

You can also delete an individual message you sent — which removes it and its recording — without deleting your account, from that message’s menu. Deleted data is removed from our active systems; routine backups are overwritten on their normal cycle.

7. How long we keep data (retention)

  • Messages and recordings: until you delete them or your account.
  • Account data: while your account is active; deleted on account deletion.
  • Crash / error logs: 90 days, then deleted.
  • Usage events: kept in limited form for product analysis.

8. Children

Soul is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect their data without verifiable parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Security

Encryption in transit (HTTPS), access controls (row-level security so users can only reach their own data), and server-side re-validation of your presence before any message unlocks. We will notify you and the authorities of a qualifying breach as required by law.

10. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes we will update the “Last updated” date and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.
